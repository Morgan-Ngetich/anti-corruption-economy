To report a corruption incident, simply navigate to the 'Report Corruption' section, fill out the user-friendly form, and attach any relevant evidence. Your report will be submitted anonymously and securely.

Yes, your identity is completely protected. All reports are submitted anonymously and securely.

You can upload any relevant documents, images, or videos that support your claim.

You can earn tokens by submitting verified reports of corruption, participating in community discussions, and contributing to the platform's development.

You can exchange your earned tokens for real-world currency through our partner exchange, which offers competitive rates and secure transactions.
